Contact Us

We have three public google groups:
1. opentreeoflife: general mailing list
2. opentreeoflife-software: software discussion (largely deprecated in favour of Gitter see below)
3. opentreeoflife-announce: announcements of new releases and features; only admins can post
All of our software is on GitHub, and you can leave bug reports and feature request as an issue in our feedback repo.
Most of our project conversation happens in a public chat room on Gitter. Clicking the logo at left will take you to the room. You can browse without logging in, or use your GitHub account to join the conversation.
