Contact Us

You can contact us through our two public google groups: opentreeoflife and opentreeoflife-software.
All of our software is on GitHub, and you can leave bug reports and feature request as an issue in our feedback repo.
The developer team hangs out at IRC: #opentreeoflife on Freenode. Clicking the button at left will join the channel as user guest, using the web-based KiwiIRC client. This is a logged channel.
